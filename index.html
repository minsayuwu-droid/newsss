Warehouse Fire: 24 Dead, Cause Under Investigation

A massive fire tore through a warehouse in Guemcheon yesterday evening, around 8:40 pm. Early reports suggest the fire began with burning motorcycles stored inside. Firefighters recovered at least 24 bodies from the scene. The tragic toll may rise as they continue suppression efforts. Authorities contained the blaze but are struggling to isolate it from electrical systems, prompting a blackout in Guemcheon as a safety precaution. The cause of the fire remains under investigation. ,CEO Park Manshik of Manshik Corporations, the warehouse owner, has not yet commented on the incident. This story is developing, and further information will be provided as it becomes available.
